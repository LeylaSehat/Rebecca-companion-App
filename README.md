# Rebecca-companion-App
We have a daughter with Down syndrome. Her name is **Rebecca** and she is 25 years old.
Rebecca is remarkably capable and handles much of her daily life independently — but she needs someone to help her stay on track with time. She also loves her phone more than almost anything else.

That gave me an idea: instead of relying on a parent or mentor to remind her of her daily routines and responsibilities, why not put that responsibility in the one place she already pays attention to her phone?
This app began as a personal project for our sweetheart Rebecca. But there really is a gap in the market:
- 200,000+ people with Down syndrome in the US alone
- 6 million worldwide
- Zero apps combining: adult-appropriate design + gamification + music theming + caregiver control + Gate Screen  If this helps Rebecca, it can help thousands of others like her.
